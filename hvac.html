Maintenance tips

Small steps can keep your HVAC system running reliably through seasonal changes.

Change filters regularly

Fresh filters reduce wear on your system and help maintain airflow and efficiency.

Keep vents clear

Blocked vents force systems to work harder. Keep furniture and curtains away from registers.

Monitor for odd sounds

Strange noises can indicate mechanical issues; the sooner you address them, the better.

Schedule seasonal checkups

Annual inspections catch small issues early and help avoid unexpected breakdowns.
